Starbucks Copycat Strawberries and Cream Cake Pops

These bite-sized strawberry treats are coated in creamy white chocolate, tasting just like the ones from Starbucks.

  • Prep Time : 20 min
  • Cook Time : 30 min
  • Total time : 2 hrs 50 mins
  • Servings : 36

Ingredients

  • 1 (15.25 ounce) box strawberry cake mix
  • 1/2 cup vanilla frosting
  • 1 (12 ounce) package white candy melts
  • 36 Lollipop sticks

Steps

  1. Bake the strawberry cake according to package directions. Let it cool completely.
  2. Crumble the cooled cake into a large bowl. Add vanilla frosting and mix until it holds together.
  3. Roll the mixture into 1-inch balls and place on a parchment-lined baking sheet. Chill for 1 hour.
  4. Melt the white candy melts in the microwave. Dip each cake pop into the melted candy coating, letting excess drip off. Let set before serving.
